I made my own orderings and made sure we had the right amount of goods in stock. I also worked at the deli counter, where I served customers. This job taught me the importance of a good work morale. I learned a lot about preparing food and different cuts of meat.

I studied at Frans Schartaus Handelsinstitut 2011. Got a degree in Store Management. Some of the courses: Team Management, Personal Management, Store Economics, Marketing and internship at Systembolaget. It taught me a lot about myself and how I interact with other people. I worked on my self awareness a lot. I also learned how to manage teams and the best way to communicate in diffrent situations. For example conflicts and coaching.


After studying at Frans Schartau I started working as a store manager. As a store manager you are responsible for achieving goals and coaching your staff on the way. You also plan and make all schedules. It is a big challenge to be manager and I learned so much, both about myself and others. My communication skills improved and I became a better listener. I belive this experience will benefit me in all my future endeavors.
